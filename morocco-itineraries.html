4 Days Morocco Desert Tour – Casablanca to Marrakech

This 4-day Morocco itinerary is designed for travelers who want to experience the highlights of Morocco in a short time. From imperial cities to the Sahara Desert and the Atlas Mountains, this journey combines culture, history, and breathtaking landscapes.

  • Day 1: Casablanca → Rabat → Fes
  • Day 2: Fes → Ifrane → Middle Atlas → Ziz Valley → Merzouga
  • Day 3: Merzouga → Rissani → Todra Gorge → Dades Valley
  • Day 4: Dades Valley → Ouarzazate → Ait Ben Haddou → Marrakech

Day 1 – Casablanca to Fes

Your journey begins in Casablanca where you will visit the impressive Hassan II Mosque overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. Continue to Rabat, Morocco’s capital, to explore the Hassan Tower, the Mausoleum of Mohammed V, and the Kasbah of the Udayas. In the afternoon, travel to Fes, one of Morocco’s oldest imperial cities, where you will spend the night.

Day 2 – Fes to Merzouga Desert

After breakfast, depart Fes and drive through Ifrane, often called the “Switzerland of Morocco” because of its alpine-style architecture. Continue through the cedar forests of the Middle Atlas Mountains, where you may encounter Barbary monkeys. Pass through the scenic Ziz Valley before arriving in Merzouga. In the evening, enjoy a camel trek into the Sahara Desert and spend the night in a traditional desert camp under the stars.

Day 3 – Sahara to Dades Valley

Wake up early to witness a spectacular sunrise over the golden dunes. After breakfast, depart the desert and visit Rissani, known for its traditional market. Continue to the dramatic Todra Gorge, famous for its towering canyon walls. Later, travel through the Valley of Roses before arriving at Dades Valley for an overnight stay surrounded by stunning rock formations.

Day 4 – Dades Valley to Marrakech

On the final day, drive along the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs. Visit Ouarzazate, often called the “Hollywood of Africa,” then explore the UNESCO-listed Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah. Cross the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka Pass before arriving in Marrakech in the evening.

5 Days Morocco Adventure – Imperial Cities & Sahara

This 5-day itinerary allows you to explore Morocco at a more relaxed pace. Discover historic medinas, mountain landscapes, and spend unforgettable nights in the Sahara Desert.

  • Day 1: Casablanca → Rabat → Fes
  • Day 2: Guided Tour of Fes
  • Day 3: Fes → Ifrane → Merzouga
  • Day 4: Merzouga → Todra Gorge → Dades
  • Day 5: Dades → Ait Ben Haddou → Marrakech

Day 1 – Arrival & Transfer to Fes

Begin in Casablanca with a visit to the Hassan II Mosque. Continue to Rabat for sightseeing before heading to Fes for the night.

Day 2 – Full Day Exploring Fes

Enjoy a guided tour of Fes including the Royal Palace, the Jewish Quarter (Mellah), and the UNESCO-listed medina. Visit Al Quaraouiyine University, one of the oldest universities in the world, and observe traditional leather tanneries.

Day 3 – Fes to Merzouga Desert

Travel through the Middle Atlas Mountains and Ziz Valley. Arrive in Merzouga for a camel ride across the dunes and an unforgettable overnight stay in a desert camp.

Day 4 – Desert to Dades Valley

After sunrise in the Sahara, visit Todra Gorge and the Valley of Roses before reaching Dades Valley for an overnight stay.

Day 5 – Dades to Marrakech

Visit Ait Ben Haddou and cross the High Atlas Mountains. Arrive in Marrakech where your unforgettable Moroccan journey concludes.
