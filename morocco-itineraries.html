MOROCCO ITINERARIES 4 DAYS HIGHLIGHTS

This Morocco itinerary shares how to have the best time in Morocco if you have only 4 days. This is a short but unforgettable itinerary. Below is a breakdown of the 4-day Morocco itinerary.


  • Day 1: Casablanca -> Rabat -> Fes
  • Day 2: Fes -> Ifrane -> Middle Atlas Mountains -> Ziz Valley -> Merzouga desert
  • Day 3: Merzouga desert -> Rissani -> Todra Gorge -> Dades Valley
  • Day 4: Dades Valley -> Oiarzazate -> Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah -> High Atlas Mountains -> Marrakech

Day 1: Start you Morocco itinerary in Casablanca with a visit to the impressive Hassan II Mosque, than travel to Rabat to explore historical sites before heading to Fes for a delightful evening in the Medina.


Day 2: Journey from Fes to the mesmerizing Merzouga desert via ifrane and the Middle Atlas Mountains, experiencing breathtaking landscapes and rare wildlife along the way. Enjoy a desert camp experience under the starry skies.


Day 3: Witness the sunrise in the Sahaha, the venture to Rissani for a traditional souk visit and explore the Todra Canyon and Dades Gorges, marveling at nature's wonders.


Day 4:Conclude your tour with a scenic drive to Marrakech, passing through Ouarzate and the iconic Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah. Traverse the High Atlas Mountains and arrive in Marrakech, ending your unforgettable journey.

MOROCCO ITINERARY 5 DAYS HIGHLIGHTS


The best Morocco itinerary for those with limited time. Planning a short trip to Morocco and aiming to explore everything? Here is how to spend 5 days in Morocco while visiting Chefchaouen, Fes, and enjoying a dersert tour. Below is a breakdown of the 5-day Mororcco itinerary.


  • Day 1: Casablanca -> Chefchaouen
  • Day 2: Chefchaouen -> Fes
  • Day 3: Fes -> Ifrane -> Middle Atlas Mountains -> Ziz Valley -> Merzouga desert
  • Day 4: Merzouga desert -> Rissani -> Todra Gorge -> Dades Valley
  • Day 5: Dades Valley -> Oiarzazate -> Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah -> High Atlas Mountains -> Marrakech

Day 1: Your adventure kicks off with a warm reception in Casablanca, followed by an echanting drive to Chefchaouen. Immerse yourself in this blue-washed gem, exploring its winding streets and vibrant markets. Relax in a charming riad, soaking in the city's unique ambiance.


Day 2: After a delightful breakfast, we journey to Fes. Explore the ancient medina with a knowledgeable guide, savoring local flavors and marveling at historical wonders. Rest in a serene riad, embracing the tranquility of Fes.


Day 3: Depart Fes for an unforgettable desert escapade. En route to Merzouga, discover ifrane's beauty and encounter Barbary apes in a cedar forest. Traverse the Ziz Valley's oasis and venture into the mesmerizing Sahaha. A camel trek awaits, leading to a luxury camp for a magical night under the stars.


Day 4: Watch a stunning sunrise before embarking on a scenic journey to Dades Gorge. Visit Rissani's bustling market and marvel at Todra Gorge's majestic cliffs. Traverse Dades Valley's picturesque landscapes before retiring in a comfortable hotel.


Day 5: Conclude your journey with visit to Ouarzazate's film studios and the iconic Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah. Journey through the High Atlas Mountains, soaking in panoramic vistas, before arriving in Marrakech, making the end of an unforgettable 5-day Moroccan odyssey.
